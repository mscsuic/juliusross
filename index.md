![A street in Islington](islington.jpg)

I am a Professor in the
[Department of Mathematics, Statistics and Computer Science](https://www.math.uic.edu/)
at the
[University of Illinois at Chicago](https://uic.edu/).

Previously, I have held positions at
[the University of Cambridge](https://www.dpmms.cam.ac.uk/),
where I was, among other things, a Fellow at
[Sidney Sussex College](http://www.sid.cam.ac.uk/),
as well as at
[Columbia University](https://www.columbia.edu/)
and
[Princeton University](http://www.math.princeton.edu).

I completed my doctorate degree at
[Imperial College London](https://www.imperial.ac.uk/mathematics)
under
[Richard Thomas](http://wwwf.imperial.ac.uk/~rpwt/)
and
[Simon Donaldson](http://scgp.stonybrook.edu/people/faculty/bios/simon-donaldson),
and my undergraduate studies at
[New College, University of Oxford](https://www.new.ox.ac.uk/)
under
[Wilson Sutherland](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wilson_Sutherland).

Since Fall 2022, I am the Head of Department.
